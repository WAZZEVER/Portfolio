Behind the Code

Hi, I’m Jemyson Ang — also known as Wazzever. I’m a 17-year-old builder based in Australia, originally from Malaysia, and I speak English, Chinese, and Malay.

My journey started with web development, where I fell in love with turning ideas into real, usable products. But everything changed when I discovered embedded systems and hardware. Suddenly, code wasn’t just on a screen — it could interact with the real world.

Today, I’m diving deep into PCB design, electronics, and 3D modelling for custom enclosures, pushing myself to build things that are not only cool, but actually work and help others. Every project is a step toward sharpening my skills and building real confidence as a creator.

My long-term goal is ambitious: to one day build a fully connected AI-powered home system. It may sound far away — but I believe great things are built one small, well-designed project at a time.

Along the way, I’m also working toward launching my own small brand — a place where I can share my projects, experiments, and ideas, and let people see what I’m building and who I am.

What I’m currently focused on:

  • PCB Design & Embedded Systems
  • AI + Hardware Integration
  • Product-Oriented Engineering
  • 3D Modelling & Rapid Prototyping
